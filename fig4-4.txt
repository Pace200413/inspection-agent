Figure 4-4. Selection of repair method for dormant cracks (after Johnson 1965)

This decision flowchart outlines the selection of repair methods for dormant cracks based on their type, whether strengthening is required, and the water condition.

Here are the complete logical paths:

**Starting Point: DORMANT CRACKS**

**Path 1: If the dormant cracks are PATTERN CRACKS**
*   **If STRENGTHENING IS REQUIRED (YES):**
    *   Then the outcome is IMPROBABLE OCCURRENCE.
*   **If STRENGTHENING IS NOT REQUIRED (NO):**
    *   **If WATER CONDITION is NONE or MINOR:**
        *   Then recommended repair methods are:
            1.  ROUTING AND SEALING
            2.  JUDICIOUS NEGLECT
            3.  AUTOGENOUS HEALING
            4.  OVERLAY
            5.  POLYMER IMPREGNATION
    *   **If WATER CONDITION is SEVERE:**
        *   Then recommended repair methods are:
            1.  OVERLAY
            2.  POLYMER IMPREGNATION

**Path 2: If the dormant cracks are ISOLATED CRACKS**
*   **If STRENGTHENING IS REQUIRED (YES):**
    *   **If WATER CONDITION is NONE:**
        *   Then recommended repair methods are:
            1.  EPOXY INJECTION
            2.  STITCHING
            3.  AUTOGENOUS HEALING
            4.  ADDITIONAL REINFORCEMENT
    *   **If WATER CONDITION is MINOR:**
        *   Then recommended repair methods are:
            1.  ADDITIONAL REINFORCEMENT + EPOXY INJECTION
            2.  AUTOGENOUS HEALING
    *   **If WATER CONDITION is SEVERE:**
        *   Then the recommended repair method is:
            1.  ADDITIONAL REINFORCEMENT + EPOXY INJECTION
*   **If STRENGTHENING IS NOT REQUIRED (NO):**
    *   **If WATER CONDITION is NONE:**
        *   Then recommended repair methods are:
            1.  EPOXY INJECTION
            2.  ROUTING AND SEALING
            3.  GROUTING
            4.  AUTOGENOUS HEALING
            5.  JUDICIOUS NEGLECT
            6.  FLEXIBLE SEALING
            7.  DRILLING AND PLUGGING
            8.  DRY PACKING
    *   **If WATER CONDITION is MINOR:**
        *   Then recommended repair methods are:
            1.  EPOXY INJECTION
            2.  ROUTING AND SEALING
            3.  GROUTING
            4.  FLEXIBLE SEALING
            5.  JUDICIOUS NEGLECT
            6.  AUTOGENOUS HEALING
            7.  DRILLING AND PLUGGING
    *   **If WATER CONDITION is SEVERE:**
        *   Then the recommended repair method is FLEXIBLE SEALING.
